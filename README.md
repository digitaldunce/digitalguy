# Digital Marketing Modules

## 1. Introduction to Digital Marketing and Foundation  
**Overview:** Understand the basics of digital marketing, its importance, and foundational concepts.  
**Tools/Skills Acquired:** Digital marketing terminology, understanding target audiences, setting marketing goals.  
**Hands-on Projects:** Create a digital marketing strategy for a hypothetical business.

## 2. Website Design  
**Overview:** Learn the fundamentals of web design and user experience.  
**Tools/Skills Acquired:** Basic HTML/CSS, website builders, user interface design principles.  
**Hands-on Projects:** Design a simple website using a website builder.

## 3. Blogging and SEO  
**Overview:** Explore how to create engaging content and optimize it for search engines.  
**Tools/Skills Acquired:** Keyword research, SEO tools, content writing skills.  
**Hands-on Projects:** Write a blog post and optimize it for SEO.

## 4. Social Media Marketing  
**Overview:** Discover strategies for effective marketing on social media platforms.  
**Tools/Skills Acquired:** Social media tools, audience engagement techniques, content planning.  
**Hands-on Projects:** Develop a social media campaign for a brand.

## 5. Paid Advertising and Google Ads  
**Overview:** Understand the principles of paid advertising and how to use Google Ads.  
**Tools/Skills Acquired:** Ad creation, targeting options, budgeting.  
**Hands-on Projects:** Create a Google Ads campaign.

## 6. Email Marketing and Content Marketing  
**Overview:** Learn about the importance of email campaigns and content marketing strategies.  
**Tools/Skills Acquired:** Email marketing tools, content calendars, audience segmentation.  
**Hands-on Projects:** Set up an email marketing campaign.

## 7. Analytics and E-commerce  
**Overview:** Grasp the basics of web analytics and e-commerce strategies.  
**Tools/Skills Acquired:** Google Analytics, e-commerce platforms, data interpretation.  
**Hands-on Projects:** Analyze traffic data for a website.

## 8. Freelancing and Career Prep  
**Overview:** Prepare for a freelance career in digital marketing.  
**Tools/Skills Acquired:** Portfolio creation, client communication, project management.  
**Hands-on Projects:** Create a portfolio showcasing your digital marketing skills.
